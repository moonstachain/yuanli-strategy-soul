## 2. YC Strategic Signal

### 2.1 Company Brain

YC's Company Brain thesis says the blocker to company AI automation is no longer mainly model capability, but domain knowledge distributed across heads, Slack, emails, tickets, databases, and old context.

The new primitive is not a chatbot over docs. It is a company brain that:

```text
pulls fragmented knowledge together;
structures it;
keeps it current;
turns it into executable skills for AI;
becomes a living map of how the company works.
```

Yuanli translation:

```text
C2 cannot stop at storing Yuanli materials. It must turn Yuanli knowledge into executable strategic skills.
```

### 2.2 AI Operating System for Companies

YC's AI OS thesis says the best AI-native companies make the entire company queryable: meetings, tickets, customer interactions, decisions, and execution traces become legible to an intelligence layer that can compare what is happening against what should happen, then adjust.

Yuanli translation:

```text
C2 must not only remember content. It must make the Yuanli system queryable and close the loop between memory, map, action, evidence, and adjustment.
```

## 3. GBrain Strategic Signal

### 3.1 Search is not Brain

GBrain's core claim:

```text
Search gives raw pages. Brain gives the answer.
```

It argues that a brain layer must perform synthesis, graph traversal, and gap analysis, not just retrieval.

Yuanli translation:

```text
C2 cannot be a retrieval box. It must answer with conclusion, evidence, missing context, next C4 action, and writeback proposal.
```

### 3.2 Answer with citations and unknowns

GBrain distinguishes raw search from `think`: search returns pages, while think composes a synthesized answer with citations and an explicit note about what the brain does not know.

Yuanli translation:

```text
Every C2 answer must include: cited facts, inferred judgment, unknown gap, next action, and writeback path.
```

### 3.3 Graph beats vector-only retrieval

GBrain emphasizes a self-wiring knowledge graph with typed edges, so answers can use relationships among people, companies, meetings, deals, and ideas rather than only semantically similar chunks.

Yuanli translation:

```text
Yuanli Brain needs typed edges among canon, module, concept, case, client, evidence, evolution note, C4 task, canonical candidate, and product offer.
```

### 3.4 Dream cycle makes memory compound

GBrain's 24/7 loop ingests, enriches, fixes citations, consolidates memory, finds contradictions, and prepares tasks.

Yuanli translation:

```text
C2 needs a Yuanli Dream Cycle: daily capture triage, weekly evidence review, monthly canon candidate review, quarterly productization review.
```

### 3.5 Schema packs matter

GBrain's schema pack idea means the brain does not impose one fixed layout. It can adapt its types and routing to the user's actual knowledge shape.

Yuanli translation:

```text
Yuanli Brain needs a Yuanli Schema Pack, not a generic people/company/deal schema.
```
